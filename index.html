Info
Because you are the coach you can do more interesting things to control the flow of the tactics training session. Your buttons are:

  • Reset

    Allows to reset the position of all of the players on the field (for you and all who is connected).

  • Lights

    Click to turn on/off the lights of the stadium(for you and all who is connected), maybe you will find it usefull to get the atention of everyone!

  • Begin/Stop Strategy

    Useful to check if your pupils know how to play some strategy. Click on play to let them do changes in their own pc. While their do it you can set the players in your pc as the right way to do it. When you click at stop, all the pupils will get your players at their pc's and can see their mistakes.

  • Online

    When the blue icon is active nobody sees the changes on the board (Play/Stop mode). When the red icon is active all the changes at the board will be seen.
